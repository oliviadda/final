My Story

All about me and how Savor came to be

How my love for cooking began...

When I was 8 years old my love of cooking and creating began! I would watch the Food Network and create any recipe the big chefs were making. I would spend hours in the kitchen experimenting and enjoying life's greatest pleasures.

I can cook, now what?

Now that I have several hours of cooking under my belt I was ready to start sharing my talents with others. I grew up with a grandma who loved to cook and loved to gather with those she loved. I proved that I could participate in creating Sunday dinners a special time of gathering and love.

Savor was born

Over many years, our Sunday dinners got bigger and bigger. People began to reach out wondering how we (my sister and I) cook for this many people and where we learned how to cook. This is how Savor was born. We wanted to create a platform of education, gathering, and food where everyone has a seat at our table.

Desserts

Desserts are enjoyed for their sweetness and indulgence, often associated with celebrations and special occasions worldwide. Whether it's a rich chocolate mousse or a refreshing fruit salad, dessert adds a sweet touch to the dining experience.

Savory

Savory flavors are rich and satisfying, often found in meats, cheeses, and cooked dishes. Acid, heat, salt, and fat play vital roles in enhancing savory flavors: acid brightens and balances richness, heat intensifies flavors, salt enhances taste perception, and fat adds richness and mouthfeel. When used in harmony, these elements elevate the savory experience, creating depth and complexity in dishes.

Salads

A salad is the best way to showcase oragnic ingredients at their ripest moments. Salads can be customized and formed into some of the greatest culinary masterpieces.

Chocolate

Chocolate is a beloved treat made from cocoa beans, prized for its rich, indulgent flavor and smooth texture. It comes in various forms, including bars, truffles, and desserts, each offering a unique sensory experience. Cocoa content determines its intensity, with dark chocolate boasting a more pronounced cocoa flavor. Whether enjoyed on its own or as an ingredient in decadent desserts, chocolate is cherished worldwide for its irresistible taste and comforting qualities.

Dough

Breads and doughs are versatile culinary staples, crafted from simple ingredients like flour, water, yeast, and salt. The process of making them by hand is a tactile and meditative experience, allowing one to feel the texture transform beneath their fingertips as they knead and shape the dough. There's beauty in the rhythm of the process, from the initial mixing of ingredients to the patient waiting as the dough rises, and finally, the aroma of freshly baked bread filling the kitchen.

Recipes

Recipes serve as valuable guides, providing precise instructions and ingredient measurements to help replicate a dish's flavors and textures accurately. They offer a structured approach, especially for beginners, ensuring consistency and success in the kitchen. However, the joy of cooking also lies in experimentation and creativity, where individuals can tailor recipes to their tastes and preferences or even invent entirely new dishes. Iconic chefs like Thomas Keller, beloved food bloggers like Melskitchen cafe, and renowned platforms like the Food Network inspire us with their recipes while encouraging culinary exploration and innovation.
